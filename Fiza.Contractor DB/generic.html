ABOUT US

The Contractor database is an application that is mainly used by the building contractors for their daily routine.Customers who are in need of the construction of their buildings need not go in person to search the contractors.On the other hand contractors will have every detail of the on going construction. The customer needs to just enter all the details according to his requirements and he may also choose the contractor of his choice based on his price.The contractor will then accept the contract and based on the contract and the number of workers he is having he will assign the workers who need to work for the contract

Features:
1.The customers need not go in person to search for the contractors.
2.The contractor will have all the details of the customer.
3.The time of the customer is saved.
